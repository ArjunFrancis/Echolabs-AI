# EchoLabs AI 5-Day Bootcamp Curriculum

## Executive Summary

The EchoLabs AI Bootcamp provides UAE enterprise teams with practical, compliance-focused training to bridge AI knowledge gaps identified in financial services, government, healthcare, telecom, and energy sectors. This intensive 5-day program delivers hands-on workshops covering AI fundamentals, evaluation methodologies, regulatory compliance, and implementation roadmaps, enabling participants to assess team readiness, select vendors objectively, and calculate ROI for AI initiatives. Designed for mixed audiences (executives, technical staff, compliance officers), the curriculum emphasizes UAE-specific regulations (NDMO, DIFC, ADGM) and generates leads for consulting services through post-bootcamp audits and maturity assessments.

## Program Overview

**Duration**: 5 days (8 hours/day, hybrid: in-person Dubai/Abu Dhabi + virtual for remote teams)

**Target Audience**: 15-25 participants per cohort from UAE enterprises (C-suite, IT leads, compliance teams)

**Learning Objectives**:
- Understand AI capabilities, limitations, and UAE compliance requirements
- Apply evaluation frameworks to assess LLM/agent performance
- Develop ROI models for AI projects with sector-specific use cases
- Build basic agentic workflows compliant with DIFC/NDMO standards
- Create personalized AI adoption roadmaps for organizational teams

**Delivery Format**: Interactive workshops (60%), hands-on labs (30%), group projects (10%) with EchoLabs platform demos

**Certification**: EchoLabs AI Readiness Certificate (digital badge for LinkedIn)

**Lead Generation Integration**: Pre-bootcamp AI maturity quiz feeds lead scoring; post-bootcamp offers free 1-hour consulting audit

## Daily Curriculum Breakdown

### Day 1: AI Foundations & UAE Regulatory Landscape

**Morning (4 hours): AI Fundamentals for Enterprise Leaders**
- **Session 1.1**: What is AI? (1 hour) - Demystifying LLMs, agents, multi-modal systems; real UAE examples (e.g., G42's Falcon models in government services) [web:59][web:75]
- **Session 1.2**: AI in UAE Context (1.5 hours) - National AI Strategy 2031, sector adoption trends (financial services: fraud detection; government: smart cities) [web:59][web:73]
- **Session 1.3**: Ethical AI & Bias Mitigation (1.5 hours) - UAE ethical guidelines, case studies of bias in hiring/recruitment AI [web:60][web:62]

**Afternoon (4 hours): UAE Regulatory Deep Dive**
- **Session 1.4**: NDMO, DIFC, ADGM Compliance (2 hours) - Data protection laws, AI risk assessments, DPIA requirements for enterprises [web:59][web:74]
- **Lab 1.1**: Compliance Gap Analysis (1.5 hours) - Group exercise: Map current AI usage to UAE regulations using EchoLabs checklist template
- **Session 1.5**: Q&A + Maturity Self-Assessment (0.5 hours) - Online quiz generates personalized AI readiness score (0-100)

**Key Takeaway**: Participants identify immediate compliance risks and opportunities in their organizations.

### Day 2: AI Evaluation & Testing Methodologies

**Morning (4 hours): Building AI Evaluation Skills**
- **Session 2.1**: LLM/Agent Evaluation Frameworks (1.5 hours) - HELM, LM-Eval-Harness benchmarks; multi-modal testing (text, vision, audio) [web:59][web:71]
- **Session 2.2**: Metrics That Matter (1.5 hours) - Accuracy, hallucination detection, bias scoring, tool-use validation for enterprise use cases
- **Session 2.3**: Hands-On Evaluation (1 hour) - Demo EchoLabs platform: Test sample LLM outputs against UAE financial services prompts

**Afternoon (4 hours): Practical Testing Labs**
- **Lab 2.1**: Bias & Safety Testing (2 hours) - Use open-source tools (e.g., Hugging Face) to audit prompts for DIFC compliance; group debrief
- **Lab 2.2**: Multi-Modal Agent Testing (1.5 hours) - Build/test simple agent for healthcare scenario (e.g., patient data anonymization)
- **Session 2.4**: Vendor Evaluation Criteria (0.5 hours) - Unbiased scoring matrix for OpenAI vs. local UAE providers (e.g., G42)

**Key Takeaway**: Teams gain confidence in evaluating AI solutions objectively, reducing vendor lock-in risks.

### Day 3: ROI Analysis & Business Case Development

**Morning (4 hours): Financial Justification for AI**
- **Session 3.1**: AI ROI Framework (1.5 hours) - Cost-benefit models, TCO calculations, payback period analysis for UAE projects [web:61][web:122]
- **Session 3.2**: Sector-Specific Use Cases (1.5 hours) - Financial: Fraud detection ROI (40% risk reduction); Government: Citizen service automation (30% efficiency gain) [web:83][web:92]
- **Session 3.3**: Risk Assessment (1 hour) - Technical, regulatory, organizational risks; mitigation strategies

**Afternoon (4 hours): Hands-On ROI Modeling**
- **Lab 3.1**: Build Your Business Case (2.5 hours) - Groups develop ROI calculator for their sector using EchoLabs templates (Excel/Google Sheets integration)
- **Session 3.4**: Pitch Preparation (1 hour) - Structure C-suite presentations: Problem, Solution, ROI, Roadmap
- **Session 3.5**: Peer Review & Feedback (0.5 hours) - Groups present; facilitators score on clarity, realism

**Key Takeaway**: Participants leave with a customized ROI model justifying AI investments to leadership.

### Day 4: Agentic AI Implementation & Monitoring

**Morning (4 hours): Building & Deploying AI Agents**
- **Session 4.1**: Agentic Workflows 101 (1.5 hours) - LangGraph/AutoGen patterns; orchestration for enterprise (no code copying, inspiration only) [web:84]
- **Session 4.2**: Integration Challenges (1.5 hours) - Legacy systems, data silos in UAE enterprises; API design best practices
- **Session 4.3**: RHFL & Continuous Improvement (1 hour) - Human feedback loops, A/B testing for compliance

**Afternoon (4 hours): Advanced Labs**
- **Lab 4.1**: Design a Compliant Agent (2 hours) - Telecom use case: Customer query agent with ADGM privacy safeguards
- **Lab 4.2**: Monitoring & Observability (1.5 hours) - Set up basic dashboards for agent performance (latency, error rates, token costs)
- **Session 4.4**: Scaling AI Safely (0.5 hours) - UAE data residency, vendor selection for production

**Key Takeaway**: Practical skills to prototype and monitor AI agents aligned with business needs.

### Day 5: Roadmaps, Team Upskilling & Certification

**Morning (4 hours): Organizational AI Adoption**
- **Session 5.1**: AI Maturity Model (1.5 hours) - EchoLabs 5-stage model: Awareness → Experimentation → Integration → Optimization → Leadership
- **Session 5.2**: Team Knowledge Assessment (1.5 hours) - Tools to identify gaps; upskilling plans for technical/non-technical roles [web:60][web:61]
- **Session 5.3**: Change Management (1 hour) - Overcoming resistance in UAE cultural contexts; leadership buy-in strategies

**Afternoon (4 hours): Capstone & Next Steps**
- **Lab 5.1**: Create Your Roadmap (2 hours) - Groups develop 3-phase AI adoption plan (no timelines, focus on milestones)
- **Session 5.4**: Consulting Services Overview (1 hour) - How EchoLabs audits/maturity assessments support implementation
- **Session 5.5**: Certification & Q&A (1 hour) - Exam (quiz + roadmap submission); certificates awarded; lead gen: Schedule free audit

**Key Takeaway**: Actionable roadmap and certification positioning participants as AI champions in their organizations.

## Implementation Notes

**Tech Stack (95% Confidence)**:
- **Platform**: EchoLabs SaaS for labs (evaluation engine, ROI calculator)
- **Tools**: Open-source (Hugging Face for testing, Google Colab for agents); UAE-hosted (Azure OpenAI for compliance)
- **Facilitators**: EchoLabs consultants (1 lead + 2 assistants per cohort)
- **Metrics**: Pre/post knowledge assessments (target: 40% improvement); NPS >8/10; 30% convert to consulting leads

**UAE Compliance**: All labs use anonymized data; sessions cover local laws; Arabic materials available for government/healthcare cohorts.

**Scalability**: Start with 4 cohorts/year (80 participants); expand to 12 with virtual options.

**Lead Gen Integration**: Maturity quiz results feed content pipeline for personalized outreach; bootcamp alumni database for newsletter.
